About this session

For the last 24 years, Steve has built his career and entire network through one thing – communication.

He started out as a flight attendant with Singapore Airlines. The job was more than just serving meals at 30,000 feet. It placed him in a room with hundreds of strangers, every single day. Very quickly, Steve learnt to read people, handle people, and earn trust fast.

That experience stuck with him.

He moved into personal branding and spent the next decade helping leaders and CEOs sharpen how they sell, connect, and influence.

After being in thousands of conversations, he noticed something. Some conversations naturally turn into deals and partnerships. Others just fade out — even when both sides seem interested.

So he built the Trust Intelligence framework to help leaders shape their conversations and close more deals.

In this session, Steve shares exactly what makes someone trust you — and how to use neuroscience to close more deals.
